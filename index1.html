Sean Stewart Brookes was born in Stourbridge. Since his birth he has been practising his guitar playing skills and soothing vocal hamonies.
His main inspirations are Bob Dylan, Simon & Garfunkel and all of the songs off both of The Flight Of The Conchords albums (Especially leggie blonde).
He has been touring slowly around the UK for the last 5 years.
He hopes to see you at one of his shows.
